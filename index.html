Five tracks from a year of tape delay sessions. An AKAI GX-260D acquired at the end of 2024 made tape-based looping the foundation for how I worked in 2025. Stacked tones captured onto an endlessly running loop. The loop left to run for hours. Signal passing through external delays, each repetition shifting the material slightly. Changes accumulating slowly. Session recordings collected throughout 2025, these five were selected and edited down into moments.

Multi-channel (9.0) audio installation
Collaboration with Twins Brand Services and Pioneer Kamaboko
Nine-channel audio installation for Twins Brand Services, presented at Chidori Bunka. Nine speakers placed among a series of Twins Brand Services inflatable sculptures, each delivering an independent generative audio stream. SuperCollider manages multi-file granular playback, generating randomized windows that continuously reorganize into an evolving atmosphere.
